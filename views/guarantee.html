Cash Back Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to providing excellent driving instruction services that will exceed your expectations. If you are not completely satisfied, you can receive a refund. The "Cash Back Satisfaction Guarantee" is our way of taking away your concerns about the quality of the driving instructions you will be receiving. The refund is limited to the cost of your 1st hour lesson fees of your 1st lesson (or one-half of 2 hour lesson fees). Requests for refunds must be submitted in writing to Complete Auto Driving School, LLC., 4301 Avenel Blvd., North Wales, Pa 19454, within 15 days of your 1st lesson. You are required to fill out the "REFUND FORM" below and mail it along with your refund request. For any reason, during your 1st hour of your 1st lesson, if you feel that our services does not meet your expectations, you may choose to STOP the lesson. Please ask your instructor to STOP the lesson, record the time and the hour into your lesson and mail in your refund request. It is that simple.
